Uberdesign is Lloyd S.

Hi there, thanks for stopping by. I'm Lloyd and this is Uberdesign. This site is where I display my portfolio, describe my services, and sometimes blog. I hope it can also help you get to know me a bit and decide whether we might work well together.

I live by a simple philosophy - "Make the most of each moment through constant growth and learning." Before completing my design education I worked as a business manager. This experience has given me unique insight into day-to-day business operations. It helps me understand the needs of business owners and approach projects outside the perspective of a designer. I enjoy the challenges which come from managing a business but feel a constant need to express my creative spirit.

I have always been interested in working in a creative field. I chose to embrace this goal by completing my Bachelor of Science in Digital Art and Design at Full Sail University. I'm also currently working towards a degree in software development at WGU.

These last few years have given me the opportunity to develop creative solutions for a variety of businesses. Working with entrepreneurs is always an interesting and engaging experience. I plan to continue developing and launching successful internet businesses, while helping others do the same.
